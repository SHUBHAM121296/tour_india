Have a look at Delhi

The capital of India, Delhi is a cosmopolitan city with a historic old Delhi and the modern New Delhi. From historical monuments to crowded shopping malls, from an extensive network of the modern metro system to Delhi University campus, Dilli has multiple personalities and is considered to be the city with a heart. Pahalgam – Jammu and Kashmir. See for yourself !

Pahalgam is a town located in the Anantnag district of Jammu and Kashmir. A major destination where snow skiing is undertaken heavily, the place is very popular among all the regions of western Himalayas. The added availability of breathtaking views makes the place a favorite with trekkers and skiers alike. The best time to visit is in the months from December to March, when the snow is nice and thick. There are ample options for accommodation.
